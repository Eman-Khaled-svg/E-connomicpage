Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you’ll receive an email with a tracking number. Track directly on the courier website or contact us for support.
What is the return policy?
You can return any product within 14 days of receipt, in its original condition with all accessories. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
Are all products original and guaranteed?
Yes, all products are 100% original with official factory warranty and invoice. We are authorized agents for major brands in Egypt.
What payment methods are available?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and Cash on Delivery. All online transactions are encrypted and 100% secure.
What is the delivery time?
Delivery within Cairo & Giza is within 24 hours, other governorates 2-3 business days. Free shipping on all orders.
