About Us

We are TechStore — your number one destination for electronics in Egypt. Combining quality, affordability, and exceptional service since 2018.

Our Story

TechStore started as a small dream in 2018 with a single store in the heart of Cairo. Our goal was simple: to provide the best original electronics at competitive prices with trustworthy customer service.

Today, after 6 years of growth, we proudly serve over 10,000 happy customers and offer more than 500 products on our constantly evolving digital platform.

Our journey continues — we are always striving to deliver better shopping experiences, newer products, and more competitive prices for our customers everywhere.
